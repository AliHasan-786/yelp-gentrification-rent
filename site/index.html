How to read this. The map shows PC1 from 30 standardized keyword frequencies; its units are relative, not a probability. “Next-year rent growth” pairs reviews in year t with Zillow ZORI growth in t + 1. Metro slopes are controlled OLS estimates per 10-percentage-point increase in gentrification-associated business share; they differ jointly (p = —) but are not causal estimates.
